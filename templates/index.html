You can create projects with list of tasks, add time goals and keep track of the amount of time that you spend in each task. You can spedify due dates to never late with any important project again. You can create teams, edit or manage them, or join to one.

Please share any suggestions you have, as I'm always looking to improve the site.

Current Features:

  • Teams, projects and tasks handling
  • Projects and tasks with due date and time goals
  • Spent time measurement, progress bar and percentage display
  • Charts and statistic on projects
  • Dark and light theme
